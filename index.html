I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Computing Science, University of Alberta and Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii), working at the Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) lab under the supervision of Prof. Randy Goebel .

I'm interested in deep learning Explainability, Interpretability, Fairness, Robustness , and the application of machine learning techniques to health care applications. My current research focuses on developing self-explainable deep learning algorithms that are capable of explaining their predictions.
